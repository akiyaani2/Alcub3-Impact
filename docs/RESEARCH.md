# Research

ALCUB3 Impact is research-informed, not research-washed.

This document summarizes the main research domains shaping the public product and open-source roadmap. It is not a full literature dump. It is a guide to what kinds of work currently inform the venture.

## Research Domains

### 1. AI Water Footprint

This domain informs:

- public AI footprint estimation
- stress-weighted impact direction
- future Scope 1 + Scope 2 reporting logic

Key themes:

- water demand from AI training and inference
- water usage effectiveness
- stress-weighted impact versus raw liters

### 2. Water Quality and Public Scoring

This domain informs:

- Water Pulse score composition
- feature selection and weighting discipline
- uncertainty handling for product-facing quality surfaces

Key themes:

- water quality prediction
- explainability and attribution
- robust scoring under incomplete data

### 3. Satellite and Geospatial Water Intelligence

This domain informs:

- Observatory workflows
- water-body change detection
- future remote-sensing product layers

Key themes:

- satellite-based monitoring
- optical + radar fusion
- geospatial segmentation
- remote inference for water conditions

### 4. Water-System Agents and Interfaces

This domain informs:

- natural-language water interfaces
- agentic querying of water systems
- public question-answering about water context

Key themes:

- LLMs for hydrology
- water-system simulation interfaces
- agentic workflows over structured water data

### 5. Data Completeness and Imputation

This domain informs:

- BasinKit robustness
- time-series continuity
- scoring reliability when public data is incomplete

Key themes:

- missing-data handling
- imputation under noisy public records
- data quality as infrastructure

## Product Translation

Research only matters if it changes product decisions.

Current examples:

- stress-weighting work sharpens the long-term footprint model
- water-agent research informs future Water Pulse interaction patterns
- geospatial PFAS and groundwater work informs the next modeled risk layers
- imputation and scoring literature informs BasinKit reliability

## Public Research Discipline

We should not surface a research result publicly as a product claim unless it has been translated into one of the following:

- measured output
- defensible estimate
- clearly labeled model
- documented roadmap direction

## Canonical Future Home

As the broader ALCUB3 research surface matures, the canonical publication archive may move to a dedicated research domain. Until then, this repo should keep the research summary short, useful, and tied to actual product surfaces.
