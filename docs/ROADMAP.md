# Roadmap

This roadmap is public-facing. It is meant to show what is usable now, what is experimental, and what is still directional.

## Status Legend

- **Live**: available and intended for use now
- **Beta**: publicly available but still evolving
- **Labs**: experimental or proof-oriented
- **Research**: under active method development
- **Roadmap**: planned, not yet operational

## Current State

### Water Pulse

- Status: **Beta**
- Role: public product
- Current scope:
  - zip code lookup
  - public water score
  - quality, drought, and flood context
  - methodology and answers pages

### BasinKit

- Status: **Live**
- Role: open-source developer layer
- Current scope:
  - public-data connectors
  - scoring helpers
  - reporting utilities
  - footprint and geospatial hooks
  - examples, notebooks, and reference benchmark pack

### Observatory

- Status: **Labs**
- Role: proof surface
- Current scope:
  - regional previews
  - satellite workflows
  - water-body change monitoring

### Water Intelligence API

- Status: **Waitlist / Beta**
- Role: hosted product infrastructure
- Current scope:
  - reporting
  - risk scoring
  - partner and developer workflows

### Accounts and Access

- Status: **Beta**
- Role: continuity layer
- Current scope:
  - progressive access model
  - account surface for saved places, alerts, and history

## Next

### Infrastructure Risk

- Status: **Research**
- Intended evidence type: mixed measured + modeled

## Research Tracks

These are active research directions, not product promises.

### Water Security Risk Index

- Status: **Research**
- Lane: Labs + Research
- Why it matters:
  - extends Water Pulse from current-state visibility into broader water security context
  - combines quality, drought, flood, groundwater, infrastructure, and climate-sensitive risk

### Desalination Siting Intelligence

- Status: **Research**
- Lane: Labs + Research
- Why it matters:
  - fits the software-only intelligence layer we can build without becoming a hardware company
  - connects water stress, coastline access, energy availability, and population-growth context

### Non-Revenue Water Estimation

- Status: **Research**
- Lane: Labs + Research
- Why it matters:
  - gives utilities a high-value operational problem before new water supply build-out
  - provides a credible bridge from public data to institutional water-loss intelligence

## What This Roadmap Is Not

This file is not:

- a fundraising plan
- a grant strategy
- a speculative moonshot list

The public roadmap should stay disciplined and reflect what is actually being built, tested, and defended.
